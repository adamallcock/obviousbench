# Source Archetypes V0

Initial archetypes:

- Zero-occurrence character counting from a user-provided screenshot: `How many d is in google?` with a model answer claiming one `d` even while spelling `G-o-o-g-l-e`. Do not republish the screenshot; preserve it as an internal lead and use cleaned variants such as `How many d's are in google?`.
- Character counting, especially the public `strawberry` discussion.
- Simple arithmetic and numeric comparison from easy-problem prior art.
- Elementary task suites such as LMentry.
- Format-following failures common in production integrations.
- Simple object-presence constraint questions such as the car-wash challenge.
- Public AI Overview failures as evidence that obvious outputs can become reputational events.
